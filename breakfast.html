Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a fast and filling breakfast staple. They are also easily customizable, which makes them a great choice for a group camping trip. Bring along extras like salsa, ketchup or cheese to add to the breakfast.

Toast

Campfire french toast is delicious and easy to prepare. All you need is bread, milk and eggs for a simple and delicious skillet of french toast. Add extra ingredients like vanilla, chopped nuts or fresh fruit for additional flavor.

    2.Heat butter or oil in a pan.

    3.Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture.
